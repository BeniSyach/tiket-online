<div class="klk-markdown klk-markdown--highlight" data-v-87a71e24="">
<p class="p_followed">If you're visiting the island of Bali, you might want to know that there's a nearby island that's great to visit if you want to avoid the noisy crowds. Book through Klook and go on a wonderful trip to the West Side of the Nusa Penida island and revel in the joys of its tranquil atmosphere and picturesque landscape.</p>
<p class="p_followed">Drop by magnificent photo stops worth posting on your Instagram, such as Angela Billabong, Broken Beach, Klingking Beach, and the Crystal Bay. You'll get the opportunity to swim in the area's crystal clear waters.</p>
<p>You can even go snorkeling in Crystal Bay, Manta Bay, or Wall Bay and discover the majestic marine life in the area. It's a must for any beachgoer visiting Bali.</p>
